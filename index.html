About me

Who am I and why have I chosen this path?

I am a graduate of Collegium Da Vinci, where I studied Computer Science with a specialization in Game Development. I have completed several projects, some of which you can see in the Projects section. In all of them, I worked as a programmer, and this is the career path I am aiming for. I truly love programming. To me, it feels like a magic pencil, but instead of drawing something into existence, you write it. However, I would never have started programming if it weren’t for my passion for games. When I was younger, it was very hard to pull me away from the computer. Stepping into virtual worlds and becoming a part of them felt incredible. That is why I chose to study this magical world of games. To turn the fantasies of human imagination into reality.
