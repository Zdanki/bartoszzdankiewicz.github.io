About me

Who am I and why have I chosen this path?

Currently, I am a game development student at Collegium Da Vincii. I have a couple projects done, some of which you can see in the Projects section. In all of them, I was a programmer, and that's the career path that I'm aiming for. I love programming. For me, it's like a magic pencil, but instead of drawing what you wish to become real, you write it. But I would not start programming if I didn't have a passion for gaming. When I was younger, it was very hard to take me away from the computer. It felt amazing to take a step into these virtual worlds and become a part of them. That's why I'm studying this magical world of games, to make a fantasy of human imagination real.
